About TEKK Bridge Academy

We're on a mission to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving tech industry, empowering the next generation of innovators and problem solvers.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of industry veterans who recognized the growing skills gap in technology, TEKK Bridge Academy was born out of necessity and passion for education reform.

We witnessed talented individuals struggling to break into tech due to outdated educational systems that couldn't keep pace with industry demands. Traditional institutions were teaching yesterday's skills for tomorrow's jobs.

Our founders, having worked at companies like Google, Microsoft, and various successful startups, decided to create a bridge – a new kind of educational platform that would connect learners directly with current industry practices and real-world applications.
